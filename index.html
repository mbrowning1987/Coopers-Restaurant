Welcome to the best Fried Chicken in Town

Are you looking for a great fried chicken joint that serves tasty, filling food as well as generous portions? At Cooper's Restaurant, our goal is to keep our customers satisfied and full of delicious, quality home cooked food that's the best you've ever had. Whether you're starving for fried chicken, fish sandwiches, mac n' cheese, or an awesome milkshake, and much more, we're ready and able to serve you.

Fresh ingredients mean quality food

We understand that great produce, impeccable seasoning, and tasty meat are what make our meals special. This is why at our chicken restaurant in Carrollton, KY, we're all about utilizing the freshest ingredients when we craft our meals. Additionally, we staff amazing cooks who know how to run the kitchen and keep things moving. Their skills, experience, and passion ensure they're always making the best dishes as well as never letting you wait too long.

Private Event Room

Looking for a private place to host your own party? We have the perfect event space that can hold up to 70 people so everybody can participate in the festivities. Plus, you can also have your delicious food conveniently catered in by Cooper's Restaurant. This space has restrooms, a TV and Wifi. Visit our restaurant today or give us a call at 1-(502)-732-4990 to place a catering or takeout order.
